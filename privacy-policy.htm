Privacy Policy

Introduction

At JwellySoft, we value the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our software and services. By using JwellySoft, you agree to the collection and use of your data in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We collect your name,address and email address when you register for an account or purchase a subscription.
  • Payment Information: For payment processing, we collect billing information through a secure third-party payment gateway (Razorpay). We do not store any sensitive payment details such as credit card numbers on our systems.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personally identifiable information about how you use the software, including the features accessed, duration of use, and general usage patterns. This helps us improve the software and provide better service.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Account Creation: To create and manage your account, including your subscription and billing details.
  • Support: To provide customer support and address any issues you encounter while using the software.
  • Billing and Payments: To process subscription payments and provide invoices as requested.
  • Improvement of Services: To analyze software usage patterns and improve the functionality, security, and performance of the app.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal and payment information. This includes encryption of data during transmission and storage, as well as the use of secure servers and firewalls. However, please note that no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted third-party service providers, such as payment processor and cloud storage providers, who assist us in operating the software and providing services to you.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or in response to legal requests, we may disclose your personal information to comply with applicable regulations or legal processes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. If you choose to cancel your subscription or delete your account, we will remove your data from our active systems, though some data may be retained for legal or administrative purposes.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal data. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jwellysoft@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
